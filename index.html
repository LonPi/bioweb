About Me

I am a Computer Science fresh grad from the University of Guelph since 2019.

I enjoy exploring new fields of computer science, developing software in teams, and discussing possible solution with programming. I mainly work in C and Python, and am familiar with Java, HTML, CSS, Javascript, and PostgreSQL. I have also made web and android games through Unity and C#.

Beyond programming, I occupy my spare time with drawing, cooking, and weight lifting.
